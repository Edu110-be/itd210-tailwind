About Us

Honda’s story began with a simple dream—to provide people with mobility. From the first motorized bicycle to the latest electric vehicles, innovation has always been at Honda's core.

Founded in 1948 by Soichiro Honda, we have grown from a small engine manufacturer to a global leader in automotive engineering.

We strive to be a company that society wants to exist by creating technologies that improve lives and protect the planet.
